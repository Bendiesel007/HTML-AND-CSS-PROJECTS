How To Properly Test your Smoke Detectors

First, find test button usually located in center of detector.Step 2 is press and hold button for at least 5 seconds until alarm sounds. Step 3 identify the sound. The alarms will sound 3-4 loud repeating chirps. If sound is weak or non existent, replace batteries immediately. Step 4,test interconnected alarms. In homes with alarms interconnected, all alarms should sound at the same time. If not interconnected, each detector will need to be checked individually. Step 5,vacuum or dust around sensors to remove dust and debris. Detectors should tested monthly and replaced every 10 years.
